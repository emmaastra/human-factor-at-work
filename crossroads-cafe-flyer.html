What is the Crossroads Café?

A regular gathering — in person in Leicester and online — where people from any workplace and any career stage come together to talk about the real experience of work. There is nothing to prepare. You just turn up and take part in honest, supportive conversation.

We talk about things like
Stress & workload Caring & work Feeling stuck Career change New management Working alone Organisational change Retirement Finding meaning Life & work balance Secondary trauma What comes next?
📍
In person — Leicester
Regular evening sessions at a central Leicester venue. Drop in — no booking needed.
💻
Online
Can't make it in person? Join us from anywhere via video. Same warm welcome.
Who is it for?
Any worker Any sector Managers Freelancers Carers in work Anyone at a crossroads
